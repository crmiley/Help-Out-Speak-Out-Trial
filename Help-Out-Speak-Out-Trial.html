Help Out Speak Out

Our Mission: We want people to pay more attention to the injustices in the world and know what they can do to create more justice. You can use this website to get ideas of things you can do to create more justice in your community, state, country, and world.

There are many injustices in the world. These include poverty, racial discrimination, wage gaps in the workplace, and anything that puts one person below another.

Creators: Maya Bernal, Nora Fox, and Cassidy Miley

Justice Ideas:

Justice in your City

  • Donate money to a local charity that supports justice
  • Hold a bake sale and donate the proceeds to a local charity that supports justice
  • Have a fund-raiser at your school, or where you work, and donate the proceeds to a local charity that supports justice
  • Spread the word about an injustice in your neighborhood
  • Volunteer at an organization in your neighborhood
  • Go to a local beach or park and pick up litter
  • If you have them, go to a town meeting and express your concerns
  • If something is not fair, organize a boycott
  • Raise awareness of international events in your community

Justice in your State

  • Send a letter to your state's representatives
  • Go to your state capital and participate in a protest or march
  • Vote for, or tell your parents to vote for, state representatives that share your beliefs
  • Work on an election campaign for a state representative that you support
  • Have a meeting with someone in your state government
  • If something is not fair, organize a boycott

Justice in your Country

  • Send a letter to officials in office, such as the president
  • Go to the capital city of your country and participate in a protest or a march
  • Vote for, or ask your parents to vote for, officials who share your beliefs
  • Pay attention to what your government is doing
  • Organize protests or marches around the country
  • If something is not fair, organize a boycott

Justice in the World

  • Create a website about injustices
  • Be aware of whats happening in the world
  • Write letters to the leaders of countries in the world
  • Become a leader of a country
  • Participate in service projects in other countries
  • Raise awareness of international events in your community
  • Organize service projects or organizations that help others world-wide
  • Donate to organizations that help create justice throughout the world
