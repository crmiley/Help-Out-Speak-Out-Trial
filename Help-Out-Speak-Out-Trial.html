Help Out Speak Out

Our Mission: We want people to pay more attention to the injustices in the world and know what they can do to create more justice. You can use this website to get ideas of things you can do to create more justice in your community, state, country, and world.

There are many injustices in the world. These include poverty, racial discrimination, wage gaps in the workplace, and anything that puts one person below another.

Creators: Maya Bernal, Nora Fox, and Cassidy Miley

Justice Ideas:

Justice in your Community

  • Donate money to a local charity that supports justice
  • Hold a bake sale and donate the proceeds to a local charity that supports justice
  • Have a fundraiser at your school, or where you work, and donate the proceeds to a local charity that supports justuce
  • Spread the word about an injustice in your neighborhood
  • Volunteer at an organization in your neighborhood
